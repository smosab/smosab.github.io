“Because Life Matters”

  MedTech offers biotechnology/medical consulting and management services in cooperation with partners including investors, biotech companies, clinical and academic laboratories, hospitals, universities, and research centers. 

MISSION

MedTech’s mission is to introduce emerging innovative biomedical technology to the global market. This goal is being attained by a teamwork of highly motivated and energetic personnel with great technical and marketing experience. 

MedTech is also committed to maintaining excellence, respect, and integrity in all aspects of our services and our professional and business conduct. We strive to reflect the highest ethical standards in our relationships with our clients and partners.

VISION 

Our vision is to become an integrated biotech company and establishing partnerships for global development.

Our Services

Engagement
We engage with entrepreneurs, community members, government officials and partners to provide the best biotechnology services to our clients.

Education and Training
With our partners we offer education and training platforms that provide a strong foundation of fundamentals and knowledge in biotechnology and life sciences that empowers our clients.

Advising
We offer advisory work plan based on 30 years of experience in entrepreneurship, medical research and the biotech knowhow. 

Sample Projects

  • Designing and managing $1.8 billion investment PPP (Public-Private -Partnership) concept plans for Medical-Health Parks in Malaysia and Indonesia.
  • Managing a project for Forensic DNA training program in a partnership with the Forensic Genetics Institute at the University of Denver.
  • Arranged autoimmune diagnostics training projects presented by Euroimmun Academy experts in Germany. 
  • Designed a comprehensive P-F-P (Public/Foreign Know how/ Private Partnership) concept plan for Libya. This concept plan was presented to the German entrepreneurs and German officials from the Foreign and Economic ministries.
